Araina Narula – Author Journey

Kindergarten – The Beginning 🌱

Araina’s storytelling journey began at a very young age. Even before she had learned to write, she would imagine beautiful little stories and narrate them with excitement.

“The Bear and The Mouse” was her very first handmade book — a heartfelt creation where she illustrated everything herself, while the story was lovingly written down.

This little book marked the beginning of a journey filled with imagination, creativity, and joy.

As Araina began learning to write, she took her next big step with “Giana and Jeeni”.

This was her first complete book where she both wrote and illustrated the story herself — a proud milestone in her early author journey.

Her ideas started taking clearer shape, and her confidence as a young storyteller began to shine.
