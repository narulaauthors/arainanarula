Araina Narula – Author Journey

Araina loves telling stories and has been a natural storyteller from a very young age. She enjoys imagining and creating new characters and worlds, especially in magical and fantasy settings. With her passion for storytelling and drawing, she began her journey as an author and illustrator.

Kindergarten (2021) — The Beginning 💗💗
As soon as Araina began speaking clearly, her storytelling journey naturally began. She loved expressing her ideas and feelings, weaving beautiful little stories around everything she explored and narrating them with excitement.
The Bear and The Mouse
A gentle story of friendship between a bear and a mouse, celebrating kindness and helping others.
This book is her very first handmade creation — a heartfelt story she narrated in her own words and illustrated herself, while the story was lovingly written down as Araina was still learning to write.
This little book marked the beginning of her storytelling journey in written form, filled with imagination, creativity, and joy.
Created and illustrated by Araina.
Giana and Jeeni
A touching story of friendship between a young girl and her beloved pet, reflecting selfless love, compassion, and courage.
As Araina began learning to write, she took her next big step with Giana and Jeeni. This was her first published book where she both wrote and illustrated the story herself — a proud milestone in her early author journey.
This story follows Giana, a young girl, and her special bond with her beloved pig, Jeeni. When Jeeni falls ill, Giana sets out on a determined journey to get her the care she needed, highlighting the deep connection children often share with their animal companions.
Written and illustrated by Araina by hand.
Available internationally, including in India, in Kindle edition.
