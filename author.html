Grade 3 — Royal Adventures Beyond the Ocean
A magical journey of courage, friendship, and helping others unfolds beyond the ocean.
The Royal Group—Princess Mia, Prince Ethan, the Witch, the Unicorn, the Fairy, and the Dog—returns for a new adventure in a mysterious land beyond the ocean.
As the Water Queen’s kingdom faces danger from her own sister, they step forward to help—discovering bravery, teamwork, and kindness along the way.
Written and illustrated digitally by Araina, this book reflects her growing creativity and storytelling imagination.
Available in paperback across India and internationally.
