About Araina

Araina is a free-spirited young girl on her self-discovery journey, learning about the world and life with each passing day. She is passionate about many beautiful and wondrous things, starting with her human connections — her family, her friends, and the people around her.

She loves to explore, observe, and experiment, creating meaningful memories along the way. She expresses her feelings and thoughts through stories, art, crafts, music, and acting.

With a constant urge to learn and engage in something new, she is gradually growing into a more confident and thoughtful person with time.
